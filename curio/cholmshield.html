Cholm Shield

The Cholm Shield (German: Cholmschild) is a World War II German military decoration. It was awarded to combatants in the Cholm Pocket on the Eastern Front from January 21st, 1942 to May 5th, 1942. The decoration was established on July 1st, 1942 with approximately 5,000 recipients. This makes it the rarest of the German combat shields from this era.
I received this medal from a friend of mine who occasionally brings me war memorabilia that he finds in his travels. I am uncertain of the date, but likely around 2017.
